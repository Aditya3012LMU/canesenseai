💰 Payment Calculator

Compute fair cane payment per farmer batch using the sucrose-fiber quality formula

Formula

Payment = W × P_b × (Pol / Pol_std) × Q_f
Q_f = 1 − α × (ADF − ADF_std)
W — weight delivered (tons)
P_b — base price per ton
Pol — avg predicted sucrose %
Pol_std — industry standard sucrose %
ADF — avg predicted fiber %
ADF_std — standard fiber %
α — fiber penalty factor
